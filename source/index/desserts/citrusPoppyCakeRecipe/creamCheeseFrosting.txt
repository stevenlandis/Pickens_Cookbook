@title
Shiny Cream-Cheese Frosting

@body
makes 3 cups

Let the frosting chill four hours before spreading.

	12 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
	6 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
	3 cups confectioners’ sugar

Beat the cream cheese in an electric mixer fitted with a paddle attachment on medium-low speed until smooth, about 1 minute.  Add the butter, and cream for 2 minutes, until smooth.  Add the confectioners’ sugar on low speed, and mix until completely combined.  Beat on medium speed until smooth and fluffy, about 1 minute.  Transfer to an airtight container, and place in the refrigerator until mixture is chilled and firm, 3-4 hours, or overnight.

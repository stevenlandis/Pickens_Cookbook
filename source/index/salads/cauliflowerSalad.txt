@title
Cauliflower Salad

@body
Paula Briglia

	1 head cauliflower, chopped
	½ head cabbage (green or purple), chopped
	8 oz. shredded mozzarella
	1 lb. bacon, fried crisp and crumbled
	1 small red onion, chopped fine

Dressing: 2 cups real mayonnaise and ¼ cup sugar, whisked together until sugar dissolves

Mix all salad ingredients together in large bowl. Toss in dressing to taste.

@title
Chocolate Icing

@body
Edith Steiner

	1 square chocolate
	1/4 cup butter
	1/2 cup cream
	3 cups powdered sugar
	1 teaspoon vanilla
	Pinch of salt

Heat the chocolate, butter and cream.  Stir until the chocolate and butter melt.  Remove and from heat.  Blend in the powdered sugar, vanilla, and salt.  Beat mixture until frosting thickens.  Add more powdered sugar, if needed.

	12 ounces flour
	12 ounces brown sugar
	12 ounces buttermilk
	9 ounces molasses
	3 eggs 
	6 ounces melted butter
	1 cup raisins
	2 tablespoons cinnamon
	1 tablespoon ground ginger
	1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
	1 teaspoon nutmeg
	1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
	1 teaspoon salt
	1 cup walnuts

Mix the first 3 ingredients by creaming the butter and sugar and then adding the walnuts.  This mixture is the topping for the cake

Mix the rest of the ingredients for the cake.  Pour into a greased pan of sufficient size.  Bake at 375 for about 40 minutes or until it is tested done.

Spread the topping over the baked cake.

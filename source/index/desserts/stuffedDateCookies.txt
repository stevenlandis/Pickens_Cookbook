@title
Stuffed Date Cookies

@body
Edith Steiner

Preheat oven to 350ยบ.

	1 pound pitted dates
	walnuts or pecan halves to stuff each date
	1/4 cup shortening
	3/4 cup brown sugar
	1 egg
	1 1/4 cup sifted flour
	1/2 teaspoon baking powder
	1/4 teaspoon salt
	1/2 teaspoon soda
	1/2 cup sour cream

Cream the shortening and brown sugar.  Beat the egg and add to the creamed mixture.  Blend well.  Mix all the dry ingredients into the creamed mixture, alternating with the sour cream.  Blend well.

Add the stuffed dates to the batter mix.  Drop each batter coated date onto a greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350ยบ for 8 to 10 minutes.  Remove to cooling rack.

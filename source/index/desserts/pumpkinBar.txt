Pumpkin Bars

@body
Another wonder of a recipe from John and Marcia Taylor

Mix wet ingredients in a large bowl:
	4 eggs
	1 2/3 cup sugar
	1 cup cooking oil
	16 oz. canned pumpkin

Mix dry ingredients separately:
	2 cups flour
	2 teaspoons baking powder
	1 teaspoon cinnamon
	1 teaspoon salt
	1 teaspoon baking soda

Gently stir flour mixture into pumpkin mixture.

Pour into ungreased 15” x 10” x 1” baking pan. Bake at 350° for 25-30 minutes, or until toothpick comes out clean.

Icing:
	3 oz. softened cream cheese
	½ cup softened butter
	1 teaspoon vanilla
	2 cups powdered sugar

Beat together cream cheese, butter and vanilla until mixed. Beat in powdered sugar in small amounts until completely mixed.

Ice cake when cooled.

	1 cup sugar

	1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
	1 teaspoon soda
	1 cup applesauce
	1/4 teaspoon salt
	1 cup nuts, chopped

Cream the sugar and shortening until fluffy.  Mix in the egg.   Add and blend in the rest of the ingredients.  Drop cookie mixture on greased cookie sheet by the spoonful.  Bake at 350ยบ for 12 to 15 minutes or until done.  Cool on rack.

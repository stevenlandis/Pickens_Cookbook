Ham Loaf

@body
Preheat oven to 350ยบ.

	1 pound ground beef
	1 pound ground cooked ham
	2 tablespoons melted butter
	1/4 cup brown sugar
	1/4 cup onion, chopped
	1/4 cup green pepper, chopped
	1 cup dry bread crumbs
	1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
	1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
	1 egg, beaten
	1 cup milk

Mix the melted butter and brown sugar and cover the bottom of a tube pan or 8 x 12 inch pan.  Mix all the other ingredients and blend well.  Place the loaf mixture on top of the brown sugar mixture in the pan.

Bake in 350 oven for 1 hour.  When baked, remove from oven, and invert on platter.

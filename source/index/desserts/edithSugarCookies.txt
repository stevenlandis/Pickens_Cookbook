Sugar Cookies (Edith)

@body
Edith Steiner

Preheat oven to 350ยบ.

	1 cup shortening
	1 1/2 cups sugar
	2 eggs, beaten
	2 teaspoons vanilla (or lemon rind)
	2 3/4 cup flour
	1/2 teaspoon soda
	1 teaspoon salt

Cream shortening and sugar.  Add rest of ingredients.  Roll batter into 1 inch balls.  Place balls on cookie sheet about 3 inches apart.  Flatten each ball with the bottom of a glass.  Dip the glass in a shallow bowl of sugar each time to prevent the dough from sticking to the glass.  Bake in a 350 oven for 8 to 10 minutes.  Remove immediately to a cooling rack.  Store in tight container.

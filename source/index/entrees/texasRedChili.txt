Texas Red Chili

@body
Chuckwagon Cooking from Marlboro Country

	1/8 pound suet, finely chopped (or bacon grease)
	3 pounds steak or roast coarsely cubed (beef or venison)
	6 tablespoons chili powder
	1 tablespoon ground oregano
	1 tablespoon crushed cumin seed
	1 tablespoon salt
	1/2 to 1 tablespoon cayenne
	2 large cloves garlic, minced
	1 tablespoon Tabasco
	1 1/2 quarts water
	1/2 cup white (yellow) cornmeal or 3 tablespoons mansa harina

In Dutch oven, fry suet until crisp; add meat cubes and brown.  Add seasonings and water; heat to boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer 1 1/2 hours or until very tender.  Skim off fat.  Stir in cornmeal and simmer, uncovered for 30 minutes.  Stir occasionally.  Serve with pinto beans or cornbread.

Serves 8 to 10.

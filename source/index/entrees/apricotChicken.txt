Apricot Chicken

@body
Rusty Jett

Preheat oven to 375ยบ.

Chicken breasts, cut in half for the number of persons to be served

	Salt and pepper
	1 can apricot pie filling
	1 tablespoon lemon juice
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
	1/2 cup pecan halves

Halve the chicken breasts and remove the skin.  The bone can also be removed.  Arrange in a 9 x 13 inch pan or casserole.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.

In a separate bowl, stir the pecan halves into the apricot filling, along with the other seasonings.  Pour this mixture over the chicken pieces.

Cover the pan and bake at 375 for 55 to 60 minutes.  Remove the cooked chicken pieces and place on top a bed of cooked rice on a platter.  Pour the sauce left in the pan in which the chicken was cooked on top of the chicken on the rice.

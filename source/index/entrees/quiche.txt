Preheat oven to 375Â°.

Crust:

	1 2/3 cup flour
	1 teaspoon salt
	5 ounces butter
	5 tablespoons water
	1 egg yolk

Mix the ingredients for the crust.  Wrap in waxed paper and let rest in refrigerator for at least 2 hours.  Remove, roll out and line a quiche pan.  Bake in preheated 375 oven for 20 to 25 minutes.

Filling:

	6 egg yolks
	2 cups cream
	1 cup milk
	1/4 teaspoon salt and pepper
	1/4 teaspoon cayenne
	1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
	1 cup grated cheese (Swiss type)
	1/3 cup bacon, chopped
	1/2 cup onions, chopped

Mix together all the ingredients for the filling.  Blend well.  Pour into the pie shell.  Bake in a 350 oven for 25 minutes.  Serve hot or cold.

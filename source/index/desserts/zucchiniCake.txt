Zucchini Cake

@body
Edith Steiner

Preheat oven to 300ยบ.

Cake:
	3 cups grated or coarsely ground zucchini squash
	3 cups flour
	3 cups sugar
	4 eggs
	1 1/2 cup corn oil
	1 cup nuts, chopped
	1 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
	2 teaspoons baking powder
	1 teaspoon soda
	1 teaspoon salt

Icing:
	1 8-ounce package cream cheese
	1 pound powdered sugar
	1 stick margarine, melted
	2 teaspoons vanilla
	1/4 cup nuts, chopped
	1 cup coconut


Mix the squash, sugar, oil, and eggs.  Add all the dry ingredients and blend well.  Grease and flour two 9 x 13-inch cake pans.  Bake cakes in a 300 oven for 1 hour or until tested done.  The cake will be moist.  Cool in pans.

Mix the cream cheese and other ingredients for the icing.  When the cakes are cooled, put the icing on them.

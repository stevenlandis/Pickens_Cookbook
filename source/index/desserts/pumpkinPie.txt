Pumpkin Pie

@body
Kroger's

Preheat oven to 450ยบ.

	1  No. 2 1/2 can pumpkin or 2 cups frozen pumpkin
	1 1/2 cups firmly packed light brown sugar
	4 eggs, well beaten
	3 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
	2 tablespoons dark cooking molasses
	2 teaspoons cinnamon
	3/4 teaspoon ginger
	1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
	1 1/4 teaspoon salt
	1 1/4 cups milk or diluted evaporated milk
	Unbaked pastry

Line two 8-inch pie plates with unbaked pastry.  Combine pumpkin, sugar, and remaining ingredients in order given.  Bake in a pre-heated very hot oven at 450ยบ for 10 minutes.  Reduce heat quickly to slow oven at 325 and continue baking for 45 minutes or until knife comes out clean when inserted in center of filling.

Serve warm or cold.

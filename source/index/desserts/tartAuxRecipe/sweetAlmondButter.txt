@title
Sweet Almond Butter

@contentTitle
Crème d'Amandes Plaza-Athénée (Sweet Almond Butter)

@body
	3/4 stick (6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
	1/3 cup sugar
	2 small eggs or 1-1/2 large eggs, beaten lightly
	1/2 teaspoon vanilla
	1 teaspoon Cognac
	3/4 cup ground blanched almonds

In a bowl cream the butter, add the sugar, beating, and beat the mixture until it is fluffy.  Add the eggs, vanilla, and Cognac, beating.  Beat the mixture until it is smooth, and stir in the almonds.  Makes about 2 cups.

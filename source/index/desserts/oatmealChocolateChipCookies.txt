@title
Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

@body
Preheat oven to 375º.

All of our grandchildren have eaten these cookies.

	1 cup butter or Crisco
	1 ¼ cup brown sugar
	½ cup sugar
	2 eggs
	2 T milk
	2 t vanilla
	1 ¾ cups flour
	1 t baking soda
	½ t salt
	2 ½ cups oatmeal [not quick]
	12 ounces chocolate chips
	1 cup chopped nuts

Do not grease cookie sheet.  Drop batter by the spoonful onto the sheet.  You set the size.  Bake for about 10 minutes.  Remove, cool, eat.

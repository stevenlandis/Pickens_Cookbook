@contentTitle
BARLEY AND LENTIL SOUP WITH SWISS CHARD

@body
10 First-Course or 6 Main-Course Servings

	1 T olive oil
	1 ½ C chopped onion
	1 ½  C chopped peeled carrots
	3 large garlic cloves, chopped
	2 ½ t ground cumin
	10 C {or more} low-salt chicken or vegetable broth
	2/3 C pearl barley
	1 14-1/2 –ounce can diced tomatoes in juice
	2/3 C dried lentils
	4 C {packed} coarsely chopped Swiss chard {or kale}
	2 T chopped fresh dill

Heat oil in heavy large non-reactive pot over medium-high heat.  Add onions and carrots.  Sauté until onions are golden brown, about 10 minutes.  Add garlic and stir 1 minute.  Mix in cumin, stir 30 seconds.  Add 10 cups broth and barley; bring to boil.  Reduce heat, partially cover and simmer 25 minutes.  Stir in tomatoes with juice and lentils; cover and simmer until barley and lentils are tender, about 30 minutes.

Add chard,  cover and simmer until chard is tender, about  minutes.  Stir in dill.  Season soup with salt and pepper.  Thin with more broth, if desired.
We have tripled this recipe.  Freezes well in meal-size portions.

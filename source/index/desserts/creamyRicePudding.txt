@title
Creamy Rice Pudding

@body
	1/2 cup sugar3 tablespoons cornstarch
	1/4 teaspoon salt
	4 cups milk
	1/2 cup uncooked rice
	1 egg slightly beaten
	1 teaspoon vanilla
	1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

In a small bowl, stir together sugar, cornstarch, and salt.  In a 3-quart saucepan, stir together milk and rice.  Stirring occasionally, cook, covered, over medium-low heat for 30 minutes or until rice is tender.  Make sure it is tender before proceeding.

Gradually stir in the cornstarch mixture.  Stirring constantly, bring to boil over medium heat and boil 1 minute.  Stir about Â½ cup of the hot cornstarch mixture into the beaten egg until blended.  Then add the egg mixture to the hot mixture in the pan.

Return the pan to the heat and, without letting the mixture boil, cook over low heat, stirring constantly, 1 to 2 minutes.  Remove from the heat.  Stir in the vanilla and nutmeg.  

Serve warm or cold.

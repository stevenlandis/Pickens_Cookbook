@contentTitle
Aunt Beth's Crab Dip

@body
Beth Briglia
 

	2 cans crabmeat (7.5 oz. each)
	8 oz. cream cheese
	½ to 1 teaspoon horseradish
	2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
	1 tablespoon milk
	¼ teaspoon salt
	Pepper to taste
	Optional:  2 T. finely chopped onion

 
Topping:  Paprika (slivered almonds, optional)
 
Mix all ingredients in bowl.  Transfer to small soufflé or similar baking dish. Sprinkle with paprika and almonds.  Bake uncovered in 325° oven until hot, about 15 minutes.  Serve with crackers – small stoned wheat thins are highly recommended!

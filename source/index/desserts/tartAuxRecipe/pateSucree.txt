@title
Pâte Sucrée Plaza-Athénée


@body
	1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
	2 tablespoons sugar
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	7 tablespoons cold unsalted butter, cut into bits
	1 large egg yolk, beaten with 3/4 teaspoon ice water

Into a bowl sift together the flour, sugar, and the salt.  Add the butter and blend the mixture until it resembles meal.  Add the egg yolk mixture and toss the mixture until the liquid in incorporated, adding more ice water if necessary to form the dough into a ball.  Flatten the dough slightly and chill the dough wrapped in wax paper for 1 hour.

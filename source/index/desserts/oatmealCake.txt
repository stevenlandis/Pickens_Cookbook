An after-school favorite

Cake:

	1 cup quick oatmeal
	1 1/4 cup boiling water
	1 stick margarine (1/2 cup shortening)
	1 cup sugar
	1 cup brown sugar
	2 eggs, beaten
	1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
	1 1/2 cup flour
	1 teaspoon soda
	1/2 teaspoon salt


Icing:

	6 tablespoons butter or margarine
	1/2 cup brown sugar
	1/2 cup sugar
	1/2 cup evaporated milk
	1 cup coconut
	1 cup nuts
	1 tablespoon vanilla


Cream the sugars and shortening.  Blend in the eggs.  Sift the dry ingredients.  Add gradually to wet mixture.  Blend well.  Pour in greased and floured cake pan.  Bake at 350ยบ oven for about 35 minutes.

Cream and blend the icing ingredients.  When the cake is done, spread the topping over the cake.  Put cake under broiler for about 5 minutes, or until lightly browned.  Watch closely so the topping does not burn.

Cowboy Cookies

@body
Barbara DuPuy

Preheat oven to 350ยบ.

	2 cups flour
	1 teaspoon soda
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	1/2 teaspoon baking powder
	1 cup margarine
	1 cup granulated sugar
	1 cup brown sugar
	2 eggs
	2 cups oats
	1 teaspoon vanilla
	1 12 ounce package of chocolate chips

Cream margarine and sugars.  Add eggs and vanilla.  Beat well.  Mix in flour, soda, salt, baking powder.  Stir in oats and then the chocolate chips.  Drop by the spoonful onto a greased cookie sheet.

Bake 12 to 15 minutes at 350.  Remove to cooling rack when done.

A note on the original recipe stated that "Mary thinks the cookies taste better with mini-chips."

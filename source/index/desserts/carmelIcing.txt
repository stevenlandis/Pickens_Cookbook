@title
Carmel Icing

@body
Edith Steiner

	2 tablespoons margarine
	1/3 cup brown sugar
	1 tablespoon water
	1 teaspoon vanilla
	1 cup powdered sugar

Combine the margarine, brown sugar, and water in a heavy pan and bring, stirring, to a full boil.  Remove from the heat.  Add the vanilla and gradually stir in the powdered sugar.  

If the mixture is too thick, add a few drops of water to thin.

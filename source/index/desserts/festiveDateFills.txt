@title
Festive Date Fills

@body
The Cookie Book, Culinary Arts Institute

Preheat oven to 350.

Filling:
	2 cups pitted dates
	1/2 cup granulated sugar
	1/4 cup water
	Grated rind of 1 small orange
	1/2 cup chopped nutmeats

Base:
	1 cup sifted flour
	1/2 teaspoon baking soda
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
	1/2 cup melted butter or margarine
	1 cup rolled oats, uncooked

For filling, combine dates, sugar, water, and orange rind.  Cook over low heat until thick, stirring frequently.  Cool.  Add nutmeats.

For base, sift together flour, soda, and salt.  Add brown sugar, melted butter, and oats; mix until well blended.  

Tightly pack two-thirds of oats mixture into greased 8-inch square pan.  Spread date filling over mixture.  Cover with remaining oats mixture, packing lightly.  

Bake at 350 30 to 35 minutes.  Cool and cut into bars.  Makes 3 dozen.

Refried Beans - New Mexican Style

@body
Chuckwagon Cooking from Marlboro Country

	2 15 ounce can pinto beans, without seasonings or 1 pound package dried beans soaked and cooked without seasonings
	1/2 cup onions, chopped
	1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
	1 clove garlic, minced
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	1/4 teaspoon pepper
	2 ounces jack cheese
	2 tablespoons butter

Mash the beans in a mixer.  Stir in all the ingredients except the butter.  Melt the butter in a skillet; add the beans and cook, stirring occasionally, until the beans have thickened.

Serve with Texas Red Chili, as a side dish, or on breakfast tacos.
